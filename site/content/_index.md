The League's Python Games curriculum is the follow-on to [Python Apprentice](https://python-apprentice.jointheleague.org/).
Students who have learned the core of Python use Pygame to build games, sharpening
their code-reading and problem-solving skills along the way. Most lessons center on
reading, analyzing, and modifying working Pygame programs, with capstone projects
that turn the pieces into full games students can play and share.
